Frequently Asked Questions

This course is one-of-a-kind, and if you don't find the answer to your question here, contact me through one of the ways in “Contact” section

01

Do I need any background knowledge for this course?

There are no prerequisites for this course (besides speaking English), and all the basis knowledge is covered in the “Introduction” lecture. This is a non-technical course, and no programming skills are required. However, each lecture has a practical step-by-step part to get “hands-on”, which is part of the course philosophy.


02

What course materials are provided?

Each course contains a video lecture with subtitles, a step-by-step guide and additional resources, such as animated graphs and executive summaries. There are different ways in which people learn, and I was taking this into account when making my own course. Therefore, it is full of analogies, infographics, storytelling, mind mapping and mnemonics.


03

How much time do I need to complete the course?

It is a self-paced course, so you can take as long as you need. Once you buy the course, you have lifelong access to it, including the additional lectures if you choose “Expert” access. Lectures are lasting between 30 minutes and 2 hours for each topic.
